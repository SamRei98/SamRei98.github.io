About me

I am a data science student from Tampere University. I am passionate about solving real-life problems with data-driven approaches. My main interests include deep learning, natural language processing and computational statistics. I have a strong professional background in applying machine learning to prediction of asset failure from sensor data. This is known as predictive maintenance. I am also interested in sports data analytics and betting (mostly in soccer). I have dabbled in match outcome prediction as well as player market value prediction. See my projects for examples.
