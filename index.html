About me

I am a data science student from Tampere University. I am passionate about solving real-life problems with data-driven approaches. My main interests include computational statistics and predictive modelling. Currently I am studying deep learning techniques for natural language and image processing. I have a strong professional background in applying machine learning to prediction of asset failure from sensor data. This is known as predictive maintenance. I am also interested in sports data analytics and betting, mostly in soccer. I have dabbled in match outcome prediction as well as player market value prediction. I also do some java coding for games and simulations. See my projects for examples of predictive maintenance and soccer data analytics. More projects coming later.

My Projects

Work-related

Predictive Maintenance Demo

A demo R notebook for creating a machine learning model for predictive maintenance. Based on a similar predictive maintenance demo from Azure AI. The notebook is in Finnish.

Promaint Webinar

A webinar about the basics of predictive maintenance given by Jukka Plattonen, the CEO of MaxiPoint OY and myself. Part of the finnish maintenance society Promaint's webinar series on the digitalisation of maintenance. The webinar is in Finnish.

Academic

Bachelor's thesis

The thesis for my BSc degree in mathematics and statistics. The topic is principal component analysis and parametric survival models for remaining useful lifetime predictions for turbofan engines using the famous Turbofan Engine Degradation Simulation dataset. The thesis was graded 5/5 and is in Finnish.

As a hobby

Veikkausliiga-statexplorer

An app coded with R-Shiny for exploring data from the Finnish football league Veikkausliiga. The data is parsed from InStat Web API and I have no ownership of the data. View code in GitHub.
